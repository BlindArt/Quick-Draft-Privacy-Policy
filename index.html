Quick Draft Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 27, 2025

Overview

Quick Draft is a Shopify extension app designed to enhance your Shopify merchant experience. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Key Point: Quick Draft does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. It operates entirely within the Shopify ecosystem as an extension.

Information Collection and Use

Quick Draft is built as a Shopify extension application, which means:

  • No Data Collection: We do not collect any personal information, store information, or user data
  • No External Servers: The app does not communicate with any external servers owned or operated by us
  • Shopify-Only Communication: All functionality operates within Shopify's infrastructure and only communicates with Shopify's servers
  • No Third-Party Services: We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or data processing services

How Quick Draft Works

Quick Draft operates as a client-side extension within your Shopify admin. The app:

  • Runs entirely within your browser and Shopify's environment
  • Accesses only the Shopify resources necessary for its functionality
  • Does not transmit any information outside of the Shopify platform
  • Does not maintain any databases or storage systems

Data Storage

Quick Draft does not store any data. Any temporary data needed for the app's functionality is handled within your browser session and is not persisted or transmitted to any external servers.

Shopify's Role

As a Shopify extension app, Quick Draft operates within Shopify's ecosystem. This means:

  • Your data remains within Shopify's infrastructure
  • Shopify's own privacy policy and terms of service apply to your merchant data
  • We recommend reviewing Shopify's Privacy Policy for information about how Shopify handles your data

Permissions

While Quick Draft may request certain Shopify API permissions to function properly, these permissions only allow the app to interact with Shopify's services on your behalf. No data accessed through these permissions is collected, stored, or transmitted outside of Shopify.

Security

Since Quick Draft does not collect or store any data, there is no data at risk. The app operates entirely within Shopify's secure infrastructure, benefiting from Shopify's enterprise-level security measures.

Children's Privacy

Quick Draft is designed for Shopify merchants and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Your Rights

Since Quick Draft does not collect or store any of your data, there is no personal data for us to access, modify, or delete. All your merchant data remains exclusively within Shopify's infrastructure and is subject to Shopify's data management practices.
